Hi, I’m Serena.
Nice to meet you!

And welcome to my personal site!

I am a junior studying computer science at the University of Washington.

I first became interested in CS after taking a computer science class in high school. After dispelling my misconceived preconceptions about the field, I finally decided to pursue CS in college. I’m so happy that I did; however, because of the obstacles I faced in deciding to pursue it, I have become passionate about diversity in tech and helping younger students to find their own passions in CS.

I work part-time as a TA for UW’s Programming in Java II, where I help students to solidify their programming knowledge. In my free time, I am working to create a mobile app. Feel free to read about my journey on this project here.

Having grown up in Seattle my whole life, I am passionate about its culture and community. My favorite thing to talk about, though, is the food! See my blog here for restaurant recommendations and more.

Thanks for visiting, and don’t hesitate to get in touch!
